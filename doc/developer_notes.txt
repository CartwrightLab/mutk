# Ideas and Notes

## verb ideas

 - mutk call: call de novo and segregating variants
 - mutk samplesites: randomly sample sites from a bed file
 - mutk loglike: calculate the log-likelihood of a dataset
 - mutk extractregions: sample regions around specific sites from a sam and fasta. Returns sam, fasta, and new bed.
 - mutk simulate: simulate mutations/variants/change reference
